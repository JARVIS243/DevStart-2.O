SQL – Intermediate 7: LEFT JOIN

Include all records from the left table and matched rows from the right

🧲 Syntax


SELECT Students.name, Marks.subject
FROM Students
LEFT JOIN Marks ON Students.id = Marks.student_id;

Returns all students, even if they don’t have matching marks.

🧪 Try This

  • Find all students and their subjects (if any)
  • Highlight students with no subjects assigned
