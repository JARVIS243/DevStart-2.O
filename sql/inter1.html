SQL – Intermediate 1: AND / OR

Use multiple conditions to filter data

🧠 Example


SELECT * FROM Students
WHERE age > 16 AND grade = 'A';

Returns students older than 16 with grade A.


SELECT * FROM Students
WHERE grade = 'A' OR grade = 'B';

Returns students with grade A or B.

🧪 Try This

  • Find students older than 15 and with grade B
  • Find students with grade A or younger than 17
