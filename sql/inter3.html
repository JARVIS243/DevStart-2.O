SQL – Intermediate 3: GROUP BY & HAVING

Group rows and filter groups

📊 GROUP BY


SELECT grade, COUNT(*) AS total
FROM Students
GROUP BY grade;

Groups all students by grade and counts how many are in each grade.

🧾 HAVING


SELECT grade, COUNT(*) AS total
FROM Students
GROUP BY grade
HAVING COUNT(*) > 1;

Filters groups that have more than 1 student.
